The Village Vet

  • Who We Are

    We are a full service veterinary clinic providing preventive care, surgery, acupuncture, herbal medicine and cold laser therapy. We are not your typical vet clinic - there are no cold stainless steel tables in the exam rooms or intimidating equipment. What you will find is a warm and talented staff who love animals and want to make sure you and your pet have the best experience possible!
